Third-party top-level page

This page plays the role of the third-party top-level document that the merchant does not control. Load it from a different origin than the merchant frame to make the cross-origin relationship real, e.g. open it via http://127.0.0.1:8080 while the merchant frame below is served from http://localhost:8080.

The iframe below embeds the merchant's own Card Fields + 3DS app: allow="payment", and no sandbox attribute.

Resulting topology: 127.0.0.1 (top, third-party) > localhost merchant frame > paypal.com Card Fields + 3DS
